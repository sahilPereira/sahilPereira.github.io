I am a graduate student at the University of Waterloo, where I work on machine learning and its applications to autonomous vehicles. I am part of the CPAMI Group and the ECE Machine Learning Lab, advised by Mark Crowley.

I did my Bachelors in ECE at the University of Waterloo. Throughout my undergraduate career I have interned at multiple companies including Autodesk, Imagine Communications, Veeva Systems and Citigroup.

Research

My research is focused on using reinforcement learning to enable learning via imitation in the domain of autonomous vehicles. I have also worked on projects related to image processing, object segmentation/tracking and game theoretic driving models. I am interested in the research and development of machine learning models to solve practical problems.
